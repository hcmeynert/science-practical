Chemistry C.1: Electrolysis of Water



Prerequisites: The electrical power supply is, again, my 3 W solar panel. The electrolyte I use is Glauber's salt (\(\text{Na}_2\text{SO}_4\)) which doesn't react with the current, just makes the distilled water electrically conductive. The graphite electrodes don't react, too, they are taken from pencils.
